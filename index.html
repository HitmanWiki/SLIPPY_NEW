About

Meet Slippy

Slippy, the energetic frog created by Matt Furie, stars in Double Fried—his own chaotic adult animated series. Known for his wild antics and relentless optimism, Slippy navigates absurd misadventures, from cooking disasters to bizarre encounters, all with fast-paced humor and surreal twists.

The show balances zany comedy with heartfelt moments, making Slippy Furie’s most iconic character. Double Fried stands out with vibrant animation, clever gags, and Slippy’s infectious charm.

How to buy?

Create a Wallet

Download Metamask or your favorite wallet from the App Store or Google Play Store. For desktop, download the google chrome extension : https://metamask.io

Get some ETH

You need ETH in your wallet to switch to $SLIPPY. You can buy ETH from an exchange or cross chain swap and send it to your wallet.

Go to Uniswap

Go https://app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask Wallet. Connect your wallet.
